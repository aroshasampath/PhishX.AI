Security Advice

How to stay safe from phishing attacks.

  • Do not click suspicious links.
  • Check sender domain carefully.
  • Do not enter passwords from email links.
  • Be careful with urgent payment or account warning emails.
  • Check attachments before downloading.
  • Use two-factor authentication.
