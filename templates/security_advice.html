Security Advice

Simple tips to stay safe from phishing emails.

Do not share OTPs

Real companies will never ask for your OTP, PIN, password, or card CVV by email.

Check links carefully

Before clicking, check whether the link domain matches the real company website.

Be careful with urgency

Words like “urgent”, “verify now”, or “account suspended” are common phishing tricks.

Before trusting an email

Check sender address Check URL domain Never share OTP Do not open unknown attachments Use official website Report suspicious emails
